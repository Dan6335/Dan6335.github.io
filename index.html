Dimensional Hybrids is about crossing dimensional planes into new worlds with new tech, & magic that helps create unique hybridized/Fusion creatures by splicing the DNA of known mobs into one that one powerful pet that's able to level up and gain skill points to unlock skills/Abilities as it levels, and they can be bred for multiple variations of textures for each hybrid species, you'll able to use hybrid for everyday life, example harvest crops, battle friend's hybrids, go on faction wars with and to Defend your base or villages, the new dimensions with new ecosystems that bring more hybrid combinations and more unique abilities. we are planning that players will be able to make a zoo, explore brand new dimensions, able to encounter new creatures, new blocks, new items, new structures, new bosses that drop displayable trophies items, new ores, new features, players able to collect all possible fusions/hybrids, all blocks and entities will have smooth Geckolib animations,.. there's so much more we've planned for the future of the mod.

JurassiCraft

JurassiCraft is a mod introducing prehistoric content into Minecraft!
This mod allows you to create dinosaurs by extracting DNA from fossils and amber, and putting that through a process until you get a baby dinosaur!
Not only does this mod add dinosaurs, it also adds many prehistoric plants!
