Bluedude Dragons

Bluedude Dragons plans to be an immersive How to Train Your Dragon mod for Forge 1.18.2 we aim to add dragons from the movies, series and books. Which you'll be able to fly, fight, hatch, and train

Features

Dragon Variants and Armor

Multiple dragon variants with the ability to equip armor.

Age-Based Scaling

Dragons have different scales based on their age.

Dragon Flight

Dragons have the ability to fly and glide.

Advanced AI

Sophisticated AI for realistic dragon behavior.

Sitting and Sleeping

Dragons have the ability to randomly sit and sleep throughout the day or night.

Dragon Nests

Unique nests for different dragon species in their respective biomes.

Dragon GUI

Interactive GUI for managing your dragons.
