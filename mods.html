Dimensional Hybrids

Dive into the Dimensional Hybrids Project, an extraordinary fusion of technology and magic across four unique mods: DH:Core, DH:Geneticist, DH:B-K-R, and DH:United. This project lets you cross dimensional planes to create unique hybrid creatures by splicing DNA from known mobs, resulting in powerful pets that level up and gain new abilities. Imagine exploring new dimensions with diverse ecosystems, battling friends' hybrids, defending your base, and even harvesting crops with your creations. Navigate the treacherous Labyrinth in DH:Core, seeking loot and relics while facing new enemy variants. Use advanced tech and magic in DH:Geneticist to craft hybrids that aid in tasks and battles. Journey through diverse dimensions with DH:B-K-R, using the Ancient Eternite Portal to discover unique ecosystems, bosses, and treasures. Finally, DH:United combines the best of hybrid creation and interdimensional exploration. With endless possibilities, smooth Geckolib animations, and a world of discovery, are you ready to embark on this thrilling journey?

JurassiCraft

JurassiCraft is a mod introducing prehistoric content into Minecraft!
This mod allows you to create dinosaurs by extracting DNA from fossils and amber, and putting that through a process until you get a baby dinosaur!
Not only does this mod add dinosaurs, it also adds many prehistoric plants!
